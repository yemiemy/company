WHAT WE DO

Accounting services are a vital component of any business, large or small. These services include the recording, classifying, and analyzing of financial transactions, as well as the preparation of financial reports and statements. The main goal of accounting services is to provide accurate and timely financial information that can be used to make informed business decisions.

One of the most important services provided by DKC is bookkeeping. This includes the process of recording financial transactions, such as sales, purchases, and payments, in a systematic manner. This information is then used to prepare financial statements, such as balance sheets and income statements, which provide a snapshot of a company's financial position and performance.

Another important service provided by DKC is tax preparation and filing. This includes the preparation of federal and Provincial tax returns, HST/GST as well as the filing of these returns with CRA. DKC also provides tax planning services, which can help Individual, and businesses minimize their tax liability and maximize their profits.

Auditing is another important service provided by DKC. This includes the independent examination of a company's financial statements, to ensure that they are accurate and comply with generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP). Auditing services can help businesses identify and correct any errors or inconsistencies in their financial statements and can also help ensure that the company is in compliance with relevant laws and regulations.

In addition to traditional accounting services provided by DKC, we also provide consulting services. This includes assistance with financial planning, budgeting, and forecasting, as well as help with strategic planning and business development. Consulting services can help businesses identify opportunities for growth and develop effective strategies to achieve their goals.

At DKC, technology also played an increasingly important role in our services, with how we are offering cloud-based accounting solutions. This allows our clients accessing their business financial information from anywhere and at any time. This also improves collaboration between DKC and our clients. Cloud-based accounting solutions is core practice of DKC to her clients.
